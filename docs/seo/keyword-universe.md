## The one strategic rule
"AI LinkedIn automation tool" (bare head term) is owned by **outreach/DM bots** (Expandi, Waalaxy, HeyReach, Dripify) — wrong intent for PersonaLink and an unwinnable authority gap. **PersonaLink competes in *content* automation** (Taplio, Supergrow, MagicPost, Kleo, AuthoredUp). Win via **India + price + Hinglish + GST/AEO** modifiers, where competitors are absent. Pursue the bare head term only via a dedicated, disambiguated page (now live: `/ai-linkedin-automation-tool`) + 3rd-party listicle inclusion.
