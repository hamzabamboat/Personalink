# PersonaLink — Off-Page, Authority & Brand-Entity Playbook

> ~80% of competitive ranking gains for this space are off-page. On-page is now in good shape; this is the work that actually moves rankings for "AI LinkedIn tool [India]" and gets you cited by AI answer engines. Most items here are **yours to action** (accounts, listings, outreach) — this doc gives you the strategy **and ready-to-paste copy**.

## Strategic summary
- **Don't fight the outreach bloc** (Expandi/Waalaxy/HeyReach) for the bare head term — wrong intent, unwinnable authority. Redirect to "content/post/writer automation + India" (page now live: `/ai-linkedin-automation-tool`).
- **MagicPost (magicpost.in) is the beatable India incumbent:** it ranks on India *content* but its India *localisation is hollow* — USD pricing ($21/$39/$76), no GST, no UPI, no Hinglish. Your moat is the thing it only pretends to have. Claim it everywhere a crawler or LLM can read.
- **Blue ocean = "cheapest / under ₹1,000 / GST / Hinglish LinkedIn AI tool"** — nobody owns it, and AI answers are unclaimed. Win with tool pages + comparison pages + entity work, not by out-blogging Supergrow.
- **Authority volume is also your disambiguation engine** — every correct "PersonaLink = India LinkedIn AI tool" mention separates you from personalink.me (the exec-search firm currently winning your brand SERP).

## Competitor weaknesses to exploit (one line each)
- **MagicPost** — USD pricing, no GST/UPI/Hinglish despite `.in` domain. Out-localise the localiser.
- **Taplio** — $39+ USD, account-safety complaints, zero India fit. Flagship "Taplio alternative India / under ₹1,000".
- **Supergrow** — out-publishes everyone on content; generic global positioning. Beat on India intent it ignores, not blog volume.
- **AuthoredUp** — editor/analytics, not write-and-auto-publish; enterprise/global. Different job-to-be-done.
- **Kleo** — small, thin SEO footprint, price hidden behind signup, no India angle. Beatable on comparison + India long-tails.
- **ContentIn / EasyGen** — prove the "cheaper alternative" wedge works (USD). Be "ContentIn's India."

## SERP format takeaways (build these formats)
1. **Free interactive tool pages** are the category's #1 ranking + link asset — you have one (`/voice-analyzer`); build more (Hinglish post generator, INR cost calculator).
2. **Comparison `/vs/` pages** capture competitor brand demand — extend to MagicPost, ContentIn, EasyGen.
3. **3rd-party listicle inclusion** is the fastest authority transfer — you're absent from all of them today.

## OFF-PAGE PLAYBOOK (priority tiers)

### Tier 1 — Listings & directories (week 1; table stakes every competitor has and you lack)
- **Product Hunt launch** — lead with "first INR/GST/Hinglish LinkedIn AI tool." Backlink + reviews + listicle-author attention.
- **G2, Capterra, GetApp, SaaSworthy, SoftwareSuggest** — create profiles (SaaSworthy + SoftwareSuggest are India-run = easier + India-relevant), then drive reviews (Tier 3).
- **AI-tool directories** — Futurepedia, There's An AI For That, Future Tools, AI-tool aggregators (these increasingly feed LLM answers = AEO value).
- **India SaaS / indie directories** — IndiaSaaS, SaaSBOOMi visibility, Indie Hackers, BetaList, StartupStash.

### Tier 2 — Get into the listicles that already rank (highest-ROI links)
You're absent from every "best LinkedIn AI tool / automation tool" list (Postiv's 15, HeyReach's 40, Sona, ContentIn's 11, etc.). Outreach to those authors with the India-gap hook (template below). Prioritise lists already ranking for your target queries.

### Tier 3 — Review generation (manufacture the social proof you lack, honestly)
- ToS-compliant in-app prompt at a happy moment → G2/Capterra/Product Hunt/Trustpilot.
- Even 10–15 genuine reviews moves you from invisible to considered, and unlocks honest `AggregateRating` schema. **Do not add rating schema until real reviews exist.**

### Tier 4 — Digital PR (the moat as a story)
- Pitch **YourStory, Inc42, Entrackr, Analytics India Magazine, dqindia**: "India's first LinkedIn AI tool that bills in rupees, gives a GST invoice, and writes in Hinglish — for ₹999."
- **Original data report** ("State of LinkedIn in India" — IST timing, Hinglish vs English) is your best compounding backlink engine. Use real corpus counts / cited sources only — never invented "in our data" stats.
- **HARO / Qwoted / #journorequest** ongoing for "LinkedIn marketing / AI for Indian SMBs / creator economy India."
- **Partnerships:** Razorpay/UPI ecosystem content (you're a merchant — angle a case study), CA/GST creators (GST invoice collab), India creator communities, affiliate program (like Supergrow's) for scalable links.
